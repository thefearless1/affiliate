Top 5 Audio Editors in 2026

For podcasters, musicians, and video creators — the editors that make audio work actually enjoyable.

Last updated: April 8, 2026

1
Adobe Audition Best Overall

Adobe Audition is the most complete audio workstation for non-musicians. Its multitrack editor, spectral frequency display, and one-click noise reduction tools are best in class. Deep integration with Premiere Pro makes it essential for video editors who need serious audio polish.

  • Spectral frequency editor
  • Excellent noise reduction
  • Tight Adobe ecosystem integration
  • Professional multitrack editing
From $20.99/mo (CC plan)
Try Audition →
2
Descript Best for Podcasters

Descript flips the audio editing paradigm — you edit audio by editing a transcript. Delete a word from the text and it's removed from the audio. Its Overdub AI voice cloning and Studio Sound noise removal are genuinely magical. The go-to tool for podcasters and talking-head video creators.

  • Edit audio by editing text
  • AI filler-word removal
  • Studio Sound noise removal
  • Screen recording built in
Free tier · paid from $12/mo
Try Descript →
3
Reaper Best Value

Reaper is a fully featured DAW for an unbelievably low price. Infinitely customizable, rock-solid stable, and updated constantly by a small dedicated team. Its JSFX scripting system lets power users build custom effects. Musicians and audio engineers swear by it.

  • One-time $60 license (discounted)
  • Extremely stable and fast
  • Deeply customizable
  • Huge plugin (VST) support
$60 discounted license
Get Reaper →
4
Logic Pro Best for Mac Musicians

Apple's Logic Pro is the best DAW for Mac-based musicians and producers. It ships with an enormous library of sounds, loops, and instruments. The one-time price and tight macOS integration make it exceptional value for anyone already in the Apple ecosystem.

  • Incredible built-in sound library
  • One-time purchase ($199)
  • Optimized for Apple Silicon
  • Stem Splitter AI tool
$199.99 one-time (Mac App Store)
Get Logic Pro →
5
Audacity 100% Free

Audacity has been the go-to free audio editor for two decades, and it still delivers. It handles recording, trimming, noise reduction, and basic effects with zero cost. Not glamorous, but reliable and available on every platform.

  • Completely free & open-source
  • Cross-platform (Win/Mac/Linux)
  • Solid noise reduction tools
  • Large plugin library
Free — open source
