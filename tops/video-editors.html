Top 5 Video Editors in 2026

From YouTube cuts to professional film work — the editors that deliver results without getting in your way.

Last updated: April 11, 2026

1
DaVinci Resolve Best Overall

DaVinci Resolve is the most complete video editor available at any price. Its color grading tools are industry-standard, the Fairlight audio page replaces a dedicated DAW, and the free version has no watermarks or export limits. Used on Hollywood productions and YouTube channels alike.

  • Industry-leading color grading
  • Free version is genuinely full-featured
  • Built-in audio and VFX tools
  • No watermark on free tier
Free · Studio $295 one-time
Get DaVinci Resolve →
2
Adobe Premiere Pro Pro Pick

Premiere Pro remains the industry standard for professional video production. Its deep integration with After Effects, Audition, and Photoshop makes it the hub of any Adobe-based workflow. AI-powered tools like Auto Reframe and Speech to Text save hours on every project.

  • Best Adobe ecosystem integration
  • AI auto-reframe and captions
  • Huge community and tutorial library
  • Multi-camera editing support
From $20.99/mo (CC plan)
Try Premiere Pro →
3
Final Cut Pro Best for Mac

Final Cut Pro is the fastest editor on Apple Silicon — it flies through 4K and 8K timelines that would choke other apps. Its magnetic timeline and one-time price make it the obvious choice for Mac-based creators who want speed without a subscription.

  • Blazing fast on Apple Silicon
  • One-time purchase, no subscription
  • Intuitive magnetic timeline
  • Excellent ProRes and HDR support
$299.99 one-time (Mac App Store)
Get Final Cut Pro →
4
CapCut Best for Creators

CapCut has evolved from a TikTok companion app into a genuinely capable editor for short-form and social content. Auto-captions, beat sync, trendy templates, and a dead-simple UI make it the fastest way to produce polished content for Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts.

  • Very easy to learn
  • Excellent auto-caption tools
  • Large template and effect library
  • Free tier covers most use cases
Free · Pro from $7.99/mo
Try CapCut →
5
kdenlive 100% Free

kdenlive is the best fully open-source video editor available. It supports multi-track editing, a wide range of formats, and a solid set of effects and transitions — all for free. A great choice for Linux users or anyone who wants a capable editor without paying a cent.

  • Completely free & open-source
  • Cross-platform (Win/Mac/Linux)
  • Multi-track timeline editing
  • Broad format support via FFmpeg
Free — open source
Download kdenlive →
